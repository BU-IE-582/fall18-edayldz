Question 1-a

In this question we are asked to analyze how good are the bookmakers at deciding over/under odds.A scatter plot of implied “over” probability average versusu the fraction of games finished over the corresponding probability range is needed for both Initial odds and finalodds for bookmakers.

Since we need to use only odds information that are in type ‘over-under’ and has 2.5 totalhandicap,I firstly filtered the data to what I needed. Then probability calculations are done. An odd is calculated as the reciprocal of the probability. For each bookmaker final and initial odds are stored in two tables.

These steps can be seen from below.

#Needed Data is filtered
Data=Data[totalhandicap==2.5 & betType=='ou']
Data$Count=NULL

#Calculation of Probabilities
Data[,Prob:=1/odd]

#Obtain last and intiial odds for each bookmaker and match
FinalOdds=Data[order(-OddDate),Count:=1:.N,c('bookmaker','oddtype','betType','matchId','totalhandicap')][Count==1]
InitialOdds=Data[order(OddDate),Count:=1:.N,c('bookmaker','oddtype','betType','matchId','totalhandicap')][Count==1]


#Probability Normalization
FinalOdds[,SumOfProb:=sum(Prob),list(bookmaker,betType,matchId,totalhandicap)]
FinalOdds[,NormalizedProb:=Prob/SumOfProb]
InitialOdds[,SumOfProb:=sum(Prob),list(bookmaker,betType,matchId,totalhandicap)]
InitialOdds[,NormalizedProb:=Prob/SumOfProb]

In order to determine the probability ranges Histogram for the odds is plotted. As it can be seen from below the “hist” function is used. As an input I give 10 classes.

BookmakersForFinal=as.data.table(unique(FinalOdds[,list(Count=.N),c("bookmaker")]))
SelectedBookMakersforFinal=BookmakersForFinal[order(-Count)][1:5]$bookmaker

HistogramFinals=hist(FinalOdds[oddtype=='over' & bookmaker %in% SelectedBookMakersforFinal]$NormalizedProb,nclass =10,main="Distribution of Probabilities for FinalOdds")

These analysis are conducted for 5 bookmakers. Betsafe, Betsson, Betway, Interwetten and bet365 are selected because they are the top 5 having the largest number of odds in the data. The histogram can be seen from above.
